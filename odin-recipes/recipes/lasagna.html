Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Minced Meat

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven on 200 Celsius for 15 minutes
  2. Put everything in side a baking tray
  3. Put the baking tray inside the oven
  4. Wait 40 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and eat
